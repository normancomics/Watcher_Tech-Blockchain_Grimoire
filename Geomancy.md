- Geomancy: Literally “divination by the earth,” a practice of interpreting signs from soil, sand, or earth patterns.
- Global Variants:  
    

- Western Geomancy: Uses 16 figures generated from random marks or binary-like dot patterns, linked to astrological houses.
- Chinese Feng Shui: Evaluates the flow of Qi through land and structures.
- Arabic & African Traditions: Interpret earth, sand, or stone configurations for guidance.

-   
    
- Occult Transmission: Tied to ancient initiatory systems, often encoded in grimoires, Enochian texts, or ritual frameworks.
